THE MERIDIAN DECEPTION — Chapter Outline

Chapter 1: The Numbers Don't Lie
Elena arrives at Meridian Analytics before dawn and discovers discrepancies in the quarterly reconciliation. Phantom billing hours across multiple enterprise accounts suggest systematic fraud. She cuts her hand on a broken coffee mug. She copies evidence to an encrypted drive and leaves.

Chapter 2: Monday Morning
Elena attends the all-hands meeting and watches Marcus present with his usual confidence. Her sister Sofia calls with something urgent. Elena discovers Lumen Bridge Consulting, a shell company collecting payments for nonexistent services. She documents seventeen suspicious transactions totaling over $2 million.

Chapter 3: The Next Day
Elena maps Lumen Bridge's corporate filings and finds the manufactured profile of David Reeves. She contacts Detective Anne Nguyen, a financial crimes investigator she met at a conference. Nguyen agrees to make quiet inquiries and instructs Elena to keep documenting without tipping off Marcus.

Chapter 4: The Server Room
Elena accesses the local backup server after hours and discovers emails between Marcus and David Reeves confirming the invoice scheme. She finds a reference to an "Ankara allocation," suggesting international money movement. She copies everything and leaves.

Chapter 5: Leverage
Marcus takes Elena to lunch and behaves with his characteristic warmth. He proposes an acquisition strategy to present to their lead investor, which Elena suspects is designed to further obscure the money trail. Elena grapples with the personal cost of exposing her partner.

Chapter 6: The Meeting
At the board meeting with investor James Whitfield, Marcus presents an acquisition target in Ankara—the endpoint of his money pipeline. Elena realizes the full scope of the scheme. She reveals she has questions about the Ankara target, signaling the beginning of the confrontation. She is prepared: evidence has been sent to law enforcement and her lawyer.
