## LIBRARY CONSISTENCY NOTES (cumulative, binding across all files)

1. **Moral architecture overrides tone register in conflicts.** When a moral register dimension conflicts with a tone register dimension, the moral register is the default winner. Tone registers can operate within moral architecture constraints but cannot override the fundamental moral premises. Override requires the tone to be confined to a single character's experience rather than claimed for the world.

2. **Pacing registers win against moral registers in structural conflicts.** When pacing requires a specific structural behavior (chapter endings, scene logic), pacing governs. Moral register governs meaning; pacing governs structure. The two can coexist because they operate at different levels.

3. **Mutually exclusive supernatural mechanics.** absent is architecturally incompatible with operative and central — no override exists. atmospheric is incompatible with central — investigation cannot coexist with uncertainty about the supernatural's existence.

4. **Protagonist structure governs character-level scene logic over pacing.** When a protagonist structure requires a behavior (the asset discovers their use; the reluctant inheritor resists) that conflicts with a pacing requirement, protagonist structure governs the specific scene while pacing governs the surrounding chapter structure.

5. **Plot engines govern story-level advancement; pacing governs chapter-level movement.** Conflicts between plot engines and pacing registers are resolved at different scales — the plot engine governs what the story is about at the macro level, while pacing governs how chapters move at the micro level. They can coexist unless the engine requires a pace the pacing dimension excludes.

6. **Nihilistic moral register denies what several plot engines require.** Specifically: restoration requires the existence of better prior states (nihilistic denies this); revelation requires that knowledge matters (nihilistic can accept this but complicates it); escalation requires directional stakes (nihilistic can coexist if what escalates is entanglement rather than corruption). These interactions are noted in each relevant conflict pair.
