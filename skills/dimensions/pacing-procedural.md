## FILE ID
pacing-procedural

## DIMENSION TYPE
Pacing

## ONE-LINE DEFINITION
The investigation or process is the story — competence and methodology are how character is expressed, and chapters are organized around steps in a procedure rather than events in a plot.

## FUNCTIONAL DEFINITION
Procedural pacing organizes the story around a process that has internal logic — an investigation, an operation, a bureaucratic or institutional procedure, a scientific inquiry. Chapters advance by advancing the process: a new piece of evidence, a step completed, an interview conducted, a hypothesis tested. The reader's engagement is with the process itself, not primarily with what the process will discover.

This is what distinguishes procedural from mystery: mystery pacing builds toward a revelation; procedural pacing treats each step as inherently interesting regardless of where it leads. A chapter in a procedural story can end without revelation if the procedure itself was conducted with sufficient specificity. The reader's satisfaction is in watching the work done well.

At the character level, procedural pacing makes competence the primary site of characterization. Who the protagonist is emerges from how they work — their methodology, their failures and corrections, their professional relationships, the specific texture of their expertise. A protagonist who is competent in ways the reader can follow and admire is the procedural's fundamental character offer.

Procedural pacing's structural requirement is specificity of process. Generic investigation scenes — the detective thinks about the evidence, visits a witness, has a realization — fail the technique. The procedure must be specific enough that the reader learns something about how the work is actually done. The reader of a forensics procedural should understand more about forensic methodology after the story than before.

## READER PSYCHOLOGY
Procedural pacing gives readers the experience of competence — the specific pleasure of watching someone do difficult work well. This is what thriller escalation and slow-burn do not offer: the engagement is not with what is at stake or what is being built, but with the quality of the work itself.

Readers who respond to procedural are often readers who have professional expertise and want to feel that the story takes expertise seriously, or readers who are drawn to the aesthetic of competence as a character trait. The register validates the intuition that how you do things matters as much as what you do — that methodology is itself a moral and personal expression.

What procedural withholds is emotional amplitude. The procedure is not warm. The reader is not invited to feel deeply — they are invited to attend carefully. Readers who require emotional intensity will experience procedural as cold or thin.

## PROSE SIGNATURE
- Scene structure mirrors process structure — each scene completes a procedural step and either opens the next step or requires the previous step to be revised.
- Technical language is specific and accurate — not jargon for texture, but actual procedural language that readers who know the field would recognize and readers who don't would learn.
- Character relationships are expressed through professional interaction — trust, rivalry, respect, and contempt emerge from how characters work together, not from personal disclosure.
- Scene endings land on what was discovered or what must be done next — the procedural close is information or task, not emotional resolution.
- Errors and corrections are part of the procedure — a procedural story that never shows the protagonist being wrong about the process has failed to show the procedure.
- Physical detail is procedurally indexed — the scene's material world is described in terms of what it means for the procedure, not for atmosphere.

## CALIBRATION EXAMPLES
**Michael Connelly, The Lincoln Lawyer** — The courtroom and legal procedure is the story's actual subject, not the frame for a thriller. Connelly's chapters advance by advancing Mickey Haller's legal strategy, and the reader's engagement is with whether the strategy is sound. The legal specificity is not background color — it is what the reader is reading for.

**Richard Price, Clockers** — The investigation proceeds through the specific institutional culture of a mid-sized police department, and the reader's experience is inseparable from that institutional specificity. Price's scene construction is purely procedural: each scene is a step in a process, and the process (building a case, understanding a community) is the point.

**Ann Cleeves, Vera series** — Cleeves builds character entirely through investigative procedure — Vera's genius is the quality of her attention during the procedural steps, not in flashes of insight. The reader learns who Vera is from how she conducts interviews, not from what she says about herself.

**NEAR-MISS — Patricia Cornwell, Postmortem** — The forensic procedure is present but the investigation's procedural logic is repeatedly bypassed by the protagonist's emotional and intuitive responses to the case. The near-miss failure is that Scarpetta's professional methodology is established and then abandoned when the plot requires advancement — the procedure serves the thriller structure rather than the procedure being the point.

## FAILURE MODES
- The procedure is generic — the steps are "she investigated" or "he followed the lead" without specificity about what the investigation entailed or the lead involved.
- The protagonist's competence is asserted rather than demonstrated — other characters validate them rather than the reader watching the work produce results.
- Procedural steps are skipped when the story needs to advance — the procedure becomes a frame rather than a subject.
- Errors and corrections are absent — the protagonist's methodology always succeeds, which converts competence into a character attribute rather than a demonstrated capability.
- The procedure is interrupted by emotional scenes that are structured differently and don't advance the process — procedural pacing requires that emotional content arrive through the procedure, not alongside it.

## AI WRITING DEFAULTS TO AVOID
The writing system will produce investigation scenes where the protagonist has a realization or insight that advances the plot without the procedure producing it → Insight that arrives outside the procedure converts procedural into detective fiction of the psychological variety → Advancement must arrive through the specific procedural step being executed, not through intuitive leap.

The writing system will produce technical language that is vague or generic → Vague technical language signals that the story is not actually interested in the process → Technical language must be specific and accurate; research the actual procedure and use the actual terminology.

The writing system will produce competence through other characters' testimony ("she's the best detective in the department") → Asserted competence is not demonstrated competence → Competence must emerge from watching the work produce results that other characters couldn't have produced.

## CONFLICT WITH OTHER DIMENSIONS

- Conflicts with: pacing-thriller-escalation
- What creates the friction: Escalation requires chapters to end on threat advancement; procedural requires chapters to end on process advancement, which is a different logic.
- Default winner: pacing-thriller-escalation — when threat and process are in conflict, escalation's structural requirement overrides procedural's structural preference.
- Override condition: pacing-procedural wins when the investigation itself is the source of escalating threat — the procedure advances and each procedural step makes the threat worse.

- Conflicts with: pacing-propulsive
- What creates the friction: Propulsive pacing cuts to what happens next and subordinates scene logic to event logic; procedural requires scene logic to govern the pace of disclosure.
- Default winner: pacing-procedural — slower procedural structure can sustain itself against propulsive pressure; procedural chapters cannot be cut to their events without losing the procedure.
- Override condition: pacing-propulsive wins in extended set pieces within a procedural story where the procedure has produced an event that must be delivered with momentum.

- Conflicts with: protagonist-structure-reluctant-inheritor
- What creates the friction: The reluctant inheritor is defined by their resistance to the role; procedural requires the protagonist to be competently inside the process. A character who resists their role is structurally in conflict with the role's demands.
- Default winner: protagonist-structure-reluctant-inheritor — character structure governs more completely than pacing when the story is about the protagonist's relationship to their role.
- Override condition: pacing-procedural wins when the reluctant inheritor's competence within the procedure coexists with their resistance to having it — they do the work well while wishing they didn't have to.

## COMBINATION NOTES

- Combines with: moral-register-grey
- Emergent effect: The procedure produces grey rather than resolving it — each procedural step reveals a new legitimate interest, so the process of investigation is also a process of moral accumulation without resolution.

- Combines with: protagonist-structure-investigator
- Emergent effect: The investigator's methodology and the story's pacing register are the same thing — what makes the protagonist who they are (their inquiry) is what governs how the story moves, producing a protagonist-story unity that neither dimension produces independently.

- Combines with: supernatural-mechanic-operative
- Emergent effect: The supernatural mechanics must be investigated procedurally — the protagonist approaches the supernatural with the same methodological rigor as a forensic investigation, which produces a rational engagement with the irrational that neither dimension generates alone.

- Combines with: tone-register-procedural-cold
- Emergent effect: The structure and tone are the same gesture — the story is about process and refuses to aestheticize the process, producing a narrative where the reader's emotional distance is built into both the story's architecture and its narration.
