## FILE ID
tone-register-dark-wit

## DIMENSION TYPE
Tone Register

## ONE-LINE DEFINITION
Humor is used to reveal rather than deflect — comedy and horror are adjacent, and laughter signals recognition rather than relief.

## FUNCTIONAL DEFINITION
Dark wit tone register uses humor not as tonal relief from difficult material but as a specific way of seeing difficult material. The humor in dark wit arrives from the accurate observation of human failure, institutional absurdity, or the gap between how things are supposed to work and how they actually work. The laughter is recognition, not release.

This distinguishes dark wit from gallows humor (which is humor as survival mechanism by people in extremity) and from satire (which uses humor to criticize). Dark wit is a narrator's relationship to the material — the voice finds the world genuinely funny in ways that do not diminish its darkness.

## PROSE SIGNATURE
- Humor arrives from juxtaposition without telegraphing — the funny element is in the construction, not marked as funny.
- Comic timing operates at the sentence level — the placement of the unexpected word or detail within the sentence produces the effect.
- Dark material is not softened by humor — the humor and the darkness coexist without one defusing the other.
- Character behavior in extremity has a specific comic quality — people doing ludicrous things in serious situations without awareness of the ludicrousness.

## CALIBRATION EXAMPLES
**Evelyn Waugh, Decline and Fall** — The systemic destruction of an innocent man is rendered with complete comic imperturbability. The humor does not make the destruction less real.

**Joseph Heller, Catch-22** — The war's institutional absurdity is the comedy's subject. The humor and the horror are the same observation from different angles.

**Christopher Moore, A Dirty Job** — Moore sustains dark wit through the collision of death as subject and comic voice as register — the comedy does not protect from the death but makes it more precisely visible.

**NEAR-MISS — Terry Pratchett, Night Watch** — Pratchett's later work tips from dark wit to dark earnestness — the humor is still present but the moral weight begins to override the comic distance. The near-miss failure is that the voice loses the equanimity that makes dark wit function; the narrator cares too much to find things funny in the same register.

## FAILURE MODES
- The humor is telegraphed — the writer signals that something is funny rather than letting the construction produce it.
- The dark material is softened by humor — the comedy provides relief rather than revelation.
- The wit is clever rather than dark — the humor is intellectually playful without the darkness that makes dark wit specific.

## AI WRITING DEFAULTS TO AVOID
The writing system will produce explicit comedy signals — "he said, finding the situation deeply ironic" → Signaled irony kills it → The dark observation must be built into the construction; the narrator does not comment on the humor.

The writing system will use humor to provide tonal relief from difficult material → Relief converts dark wit to tonal variation → The humor must exist alongside the darkness without defusing it.
