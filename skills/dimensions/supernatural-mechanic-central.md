## FILE ID
supernatural-mechanic-central

## DIMENSION TYPE
Supernatural Mechanic

## ONE-LINE DEFINITION
The supernatural is the story's subject — its nature, rules, costs, and implications for human experience are what the narrative is structured to investigate.

## FUNCTIONAL DEFINITION
Central supernatural mechanic makes the supernatural not just real but the story's primary subject matter. The story is about the supernatural: how it works, what it costs, what it reveals about the world or human nature, what happens when human beings are exposed to it or incorporate it into their lives. The plot, characters, and themes are organized in service of the investigation of the supernatural.

This is different from operative: operative uses the supernatural as infrastructure within a story that is about something else. Central makes the investigation of the supernatural infrastructure itself the story's subject. The protagonist's central question is a question about the supernatural — what is it, what does it want, what are its rules, what does it mean.

At the structural level, central supernatural organizes the story around progressive revelation: each act reveals something new about the supernatural that complicates what was previously understood. The story's architecture is the architecture of deepening understanding — the reader and protagonist come to know the supernatural more completely as the story progresses, and each new understanding changes the terms of the story's central question.

The challenge is that stories organized around investigation of a subject must have a subject that can sustain investigation. A supernatural element that is immediately comprehensible cannot support a central mechanic — the investigation would end too soon.

## READER PSYCHOLOGY
Central supernatural mechanic gives readers the experience of inhabiting a story where the rules of the world are genuinely uncertain in ways that matter. The reader is in a state of sustained inquiry about the nature of the supernatural alongside the protagonist — each revelation reframes what came before and opens new questions.

Readers who respond to central supernatural want the supernatural to be genuinely strange and genuinely significant — not infrastructure or atmosphere, but a force that changes what the world means. The register invites the reader to think about the supernatural as seriously as the story does.

What central withholds is the comfort of established rules. Unlike operative, central supernatural can and usually does have its rules revised, complicated, or revealed as incomplete as the story progresses. Readers who want a stable rule-governed world will find central mechanic unsettling.

## PROSE SIGNATURE
- Scenes that reveal new information about the supernatural are given more narrative space and weight than scenes that advance the plot — the investigation of the supernatural IS the primary forward movement.
- Character response to supernatural revelations is extended and consequential — the protagonist must process what they have learned about the supernatural before the story continues, because what they have learned changes the terms.
- The supernatural's rules are presented and then complicated — things that seemed to be rules are revealed as partial or conditional.
- Physical description of the supernatural is specific and original — not drawn from genre convention but built for this story's specific supernatural.
- The story's central question about the supernatural is kept open even as partial answers arrive — each answer generates new questions.
- The protagonist's relationship to the supernatural is dynamic — their understanding, fear, wonder, or acceptance evolves as the investigation deepens.

## CALIBRATION EXAMPLES
**Jeff VanderMeer, Annihilation** — The Southern Reach trilogy makes the nature of Area X the story's subject. Every scene advances the investigation of what Area X is. VanderMeer's prose builds the supernatural through the specific texture of what it does to people and environments rather than through exposition, and each investigation reveals something that complicates the previous understanding.

**N.K. Jemisin, The Fifth Season** — The orogeny system is the story's subject — what it is, where it came from, what it costs, and what it means for the people who have it and the society organized around controlling them. Jemisin's investigation of orogeny's history and implications is the story's structural spine.

**Silvia Moreno-Garcia, Mexican Gothic** — The house's supernatural nature is what the novel investigates. Each chapter advances the protagonist's understanding of what the house is and what it wants. Moreno-Garcia's prose renders the investigation as a systematic, if increasingly disturbing, inquiry.

**NEAR-MISS — Deborah Harkness, A Discovery of Witches** — The novel presents itself as an investigation of the origins of supernatural creatures, which suggests central mechanic, but the investigation is abandoned for romantic and domestic plot in the middle third. The near-miss failure is that the supernatural investigation is displaced by the romance rather than sustained as the story's subject — the mechanic slides from central to operative and then to atmospheric.

## FAILURE MODES
- The investigation of the supernatural is completed too early — the story provides a comprehensive answer before the narrative has built the question thoroughly.
- The supernatural investigation is displaced by other plot elements that become more interesting than the supernatural — the story drifts from central to operative.
- The supernatural is revealed to have a prosaic explanation that reduces it to absent mechanic territory — the investigation concludes by disproving the supernatural.
- The supernatural is described through genre convention rather than through original construction — the reader's prior knowledge of vampire or ghost conventions substitutes for genuine investigation.
- The protagonist's understanding of the supernatural does not change through the investigation — they end where they began, and the investigation produced no genuine revelation.

## AI WRITING DEFAULTS TO AVOID
The writing system will establish the supernatural's rules early and then apply them consistently → Complete early establishment converts central to operative → The supernatural's rules must be established provisionally and then complicated — what seemed like a rule must be revealed as partial or conditional.

The writing system will produce supernatural scenes that advance plot rather than advance understanding of the supernatural → Plot-advancing supernatural is operative, not central → Supernatural scenes in central mechanic advance the investigation of the supernatural's nature; plot advancement is secondary.

The writing system will describe the supernatural through genre-conventional imagery → Genre convention substitutes pre-existing reader knowledge for genuine investigation → The supernatural's physical reality must be built from original description that this story owns.

## CONFLICT WITH OTHER DIMENSIONS

- Conflicts with: supernatural-mechanic-absent
- What creates the friction: Absent denies the supernatural; central makes it the story's subject.
- Default winner: supernatural-mechanic-central — mutually exclusive.
- Override condition: None.

- Conflicts with: supernatural-mechanic-atmospheric
- What creates the friction: Atmospheric refuses to confirm the supernatural; central is organized around progressively understanding it.
- Default winner: supernatural-mechanic-central — investigation requires confirmation; atmospheric refuses it.
- Override condition: None — investigation of a thing's nature requires the thing to exist.

- Conflicts with: pacing-propulsive
- What creates the friction: Propulsive pacing cuts to what happens next; central supernatural requires extended scenes of investigation and processing that propulsive cuts.
- Default winner: supernatural-mechanic-central — the story's subject governs its pace more completely than its pacing dimension; central supernatural requires the space to investigate.
- Override condition: pacing-propulsive wins in sections where the investigation is producing rapid successive revelations — propulsive pacing can sustain central mechanic when the investigation itself is moving fast.

## COMBINATION NOTES

- Combines with: moral-register-nihilistic
- Emergent effect: The supernatural, fully understood, does not operate along moral lines — the investigation confirms that the supernatural is indifferent to human ethics, which makes the investigation itself a nihilistic act, producing a story where the act of knowing is the act of disillusionment.

- Combines with: protagonist-structure-investigator
- Emergent effect: The investigator's methodology becomes the narrative structure — the investigation of the supernatural proceeds through the investigator's specific approach to inquiry, and the story is simultaneously about what is found and about the method of finding it.

- Combines with: pacing-slow-burn
- Emergent effect: The investigation of the supernatural proceeds through accumulation rather than revelation — each scene adds a piece of understanding without providing the full picture, until the accumulated understanding produces a comprehension that no single revelation could have delivered.

- Combines with: plot-engine-revelation
- Emergent effect: The revelation that recontextualizes everything is a revelation about the supernatural's nature — the plot engine and the supernatural mechanic are aligned, so the story's structural climax is the moment when the investigation of the supernatural produces its most complete and disturbing answer.
